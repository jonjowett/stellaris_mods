This mod updates the number of available ascension perks to 16, and adds 8 levels of repeatable tech so that you can unlock all of the slots.

[h1]Technical info / compatibility:[/h1]
[list][*]This mod should be compatible with any other mod that doesn't alter the traditions view or NGameplay -> ASCENSION_PERKS_SLOTS in defines.[/list]
